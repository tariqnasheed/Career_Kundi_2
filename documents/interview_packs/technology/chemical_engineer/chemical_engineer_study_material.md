Hazmat safety is where chemical knowledge becomes operational discipline. At a specialty chemicals unit we had recurring near-miss vapor alarms during drum-to-reactor solvent charging. We also segregated oxidizer drums to a separate bay with dedicated spill bund. In the next 12 months there were zero vapor alarms during charging and insurer audit findings closed without conditions.
