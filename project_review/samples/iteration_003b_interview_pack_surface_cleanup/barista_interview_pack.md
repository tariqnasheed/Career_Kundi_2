## Role overview
The Barista role integrates Coffee preparation, Cash handling, Customer service to deliver on responsibilities such as prepare drinks to standard. Employers at the Entry level (0–2 years) level expect candidates to demonstrate both conceptual depth and applied experience — not surface familiarity with keywords.
**Key responsibilities**
- Prepare drinks to standard
- Operate POS
- Keep workspace clean
**Required skills:** Coffee preparation, Cash handling, Customer service

- Reference professional standards, regulations, or best-practice frameworks appropriate to this field.
